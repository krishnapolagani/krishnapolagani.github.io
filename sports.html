Sports & Fitness

Passion, energy, discipline, and inspiration through sports.

Sports have always been a big part of my life — not just entertainment, but a source of motivation, teamwork, focus, and resilience. Whether it's the strategy in a tight cricket chase, the atmosphere of a Blue Jays game, or the precision of Formula 1 racing, every sport brings its own excitement and lessons.

Interests

Sports I follow or play

Cricket – Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH)

I'm a dedicated supporter of SRH. I enjoy the strategic side of T20 cricket — from powerplay momentum to death-over execution. SRH's bowling strength and comeback performances always keep matches exciting.

Baseball – Toronto Blue Jays

Watching the Blue Jays introduced me to baseball's tactical depth. I enjoy the pitching battles, big home-run moments, and the electric atmosphere at Rogers Centre.

Formula 1 – Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen’s driving style — aggressive, calculated, and relentlessly competitive — makes every race exciting. F1 combines engineering excellence with raw driver skill.
