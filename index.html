Profile

About

Who I am and how I work.

I am a PLM Techno-functional Architect and Business Systems Analyst with more than fourteen years of experience delivering PLM implementations and integrations across medical devices, hi-tech, aerospace, semiconductor, industrial equipment, consumer goods and healthcare.

My core strengths are in Siemens Teamcenter, Oracle Agile PLM, PTC Windchill and MechWorks PDM, combined with hands on integration work using T4X and T4O, Oracle Fusion Middleware, APIs and Power Platform. I design integration patterns that connect PLM with ERP systems such as Oracle EBS, Syspro, SAP and Microsoft Dynamics, as well as ALM, MES and QMS, to create a reliable digital thread.

I am comfortable working between business and IT: leading workshops, clarifying requirements, running fit gap analysis and then working with technical teams to make sure solutions are practical, testable and supportable. I like simplifying complex landscapes and aligning tools, data and processes so teams can focus on delivery instead of constantly firefighting integrations.

Outside work I enjoy travelling and following sports. Both of these influence how I think about teamwork, resilience and continuous improvement when working with cross functional and global teams.
