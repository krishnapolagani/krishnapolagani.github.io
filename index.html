About

A quick view of my background and how I like to work.

I’m a PLM techno-functional architect and business systems analyst with over fourteen years of experience delivering PLM implementations and integrations across medical devices, hi-tech, aerospace, semiconductors, industrial equipment, consumer goods and healthcare.

My core expertise covers Siemens Teamcenter, Oracle Agile PLM, PTC Windchill and MechWorks PDM, supported by hands-on integration experience using T4X/T4O, Oracle Fusion Middleware, APIs and Power Platform. I design integration patterns that connect PLM with ERP systems such as Oracle EBS, Syspro, SAP and Microsoft Dynamics, as well as ALM, MES and QMS solutions.

I enjoy working in the space between business and technology, running workshops, clarifying requirements, performing fit-gap analysis and working with technical teams to ensure solutions are practical, testable and supportable. I like simplifying complex landscapes and aligning tools, data and processes so teams can deliver without constantly firefighting integrations.

Outside work I enjoy travelling and following sports. Both shape how I think about collaboration, resilience and continuous improvement when working with cross-functional and global teams.
